---

Intealth primarily focuses on delivering services to international medical graduates (IMGs) throughout every stage of their career. This begins when they graduate from medical school and are preparing to enter graduate medical education (GME), and continues through licensure and medical practice.

## Learn more

- [Intealth Home Page](https://intealth.org/)
- [ECFMG Home Page](https://www.ecfmg.org/)

Intealth employs case managers who work directly with these medical professionals and help deliver the services provided through ECFMG.

> 💡 The way Intealth provides services has a major impact on the technologies it uses. See [Conway’s Law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conway%27s_law).
