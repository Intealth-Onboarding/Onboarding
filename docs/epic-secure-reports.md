# Overview

**EPIC: A service for verifying medical credentials**

- EPIC is a web-based platform that allows physicians and organizations to verify the authenticity of medical credentials related to education, training, and registration/licensure.

- EPIC uses the same primary-source verification process as ECFMG, which confirms the authenticity of credentials directly with the issuing institutions.

- EPIC helps physicians build a digital career portfolio of their verified credentials, which they can share with organizations worldwide for various opportunities.

- EPIC provides organizations access to primary-source verified credentials of their physician applicants, at no cost, through a secure portal.
