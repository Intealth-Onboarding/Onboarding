## What you can expect from your co-op experience at Intealth

- Co-ops are not treated as a sub-class of an employee.
- You will be given as much responsibility as you can take on.
- Your efforts will be used in production systems.
- The team is there to support you; you will learn a lot, and you will be challenged.

---

## What is expected of you

### Active Participation

- Active participation in 2x daily scrum meetings
- Come prepared with a succinct summary of:
  - what you’ve done
  - what you will do
  - any problems preventing you from moving forward

---

### Independence

- Try to find solutions on your own, but ask for help if you’re not getting anywhere

> 💡 If you spend more than one day on the same issue, ask for guidance on finding a solution.  
> The team won’t likely give direct answers, but instead will help guide you to finding it yourself so you develop your problem-solving skills.

Be sure to check back to this site for learning resources.

---

### Question things

- The team benefits from many different viewpoints
- Your opinion matters
- If you don’t understand why things are done a certain way, or have a suggestion for a different or better approach, you should feel free to speak up and engage in conversation
