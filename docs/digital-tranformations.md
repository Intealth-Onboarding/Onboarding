## Digital Transformation at Intealth

Intealth has been on a multi-year journey to modernize its technical infrastructure and has re-platformed much of it to Salesforce.

> 💡 Read more: [Transition to MyIntealth](https://www.ecfmg.org/myintealth/)

---

## Salesforce

Intealth’s Salesforce implementation leverages several products to deliver services across the organization.

### Salesforce Products / Services

- **Salesforce Service Cloud** — Case Management (used by case workers at Intealth)
- **Salesforce Community Cloud** — Applicant Services
- **Salesforce Community Cloud** — Entity Services
- **Non-Profit Success Pack (NPSP)** — Household accounts, fundraising, etc.
- **MuleSoft** — Integration between Salesforce and other platforms
- **Tableau** — Reporting and analytics
- **Heroku PostgreSQL** — Relational database functionality

---

### 3rd Party Integrations

- **PDFTron** — PDF handling in browser (annotations, merges, etc.)
- **Accounting Seed** — General ledger + Great Plains integration
- **Kofax Capture** — Document scanning
- ...and many more
